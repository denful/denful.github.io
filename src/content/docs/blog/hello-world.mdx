This is the first post on the Denful blog.

For a long time these libraries lived under my github.com/vic profile, and the writing about them lived in scattered READMEs. Denful is a community now, vic is just an small portion of it, so the writing should live somewhere shared. That is what this blog is for.

## What goes here

Whatever is worth sharing. A release and why it matters. How gen* libraries can enable other Nix tooling besides Den. A pattern in Nix that took us too long to find. Notes from building dnx. Short is fine, technical is welcome, anything that helps Nix people.

## Who can write

If you build Denful, this is yours too. This space is for anyone who has contributed: open a post and say what you are thinking about. New contributors are just as welcome, and you do not need permission.

## How to add a post

Create a file at `src/content/docs/blog/your-title.mdx` with frontmatter:

```yaml
---
title: Your Title
description: One line summary
---
```

Then write in plain Markdown, build locally to check it renders, and open a pull request.

That is it. More to come.
