[ranix](https://github.com/denful/ranix) is a Nix language parser implemented in C, built with **ragel** (scanner) and **bison** (grammar).
</Aside>

## What it does

ranix parses the Nix language in C. It uses ragel to generate the lexical scanner and bison to generate the grammar parser, a classic, fast C toolchain for turning Nix source into a syntax tree.

## Role in the ecosystem

ranix sits at the front of the Nix-evaluation exploration. A low-level, portable parser that complements the interaction-net engines ([GoDNet](/ecosystem/godnet/), [GoDNix](/ecosystem/godnix/)) and the [dnx](/ecosystem/dnx/) runtime.
