**denful** provides a catalog of ready made [Den](/ecosystem/den/) aspects that you can mix into your Aspect oriented configurations. Think of it as a LazyVim like distribution: battle tested defaults for common use cases, so you spend less time configuring and more time building.

## Features

- **No Nix wizardry required**: designed for beginners, but extensible for experts
- **Minimalistic and interoperable**: incrementally enable just what you need
- **All module classes**: supports NixOS, Darwin, Home-Manager, and more
- **Multi host, multi user**: high module reuse across configurations
- **Secrets management**: built in support
- **Automatic documentation**: self documenting facets
- **Flake agnostic**: experimental support for non flake usage

## Role in the Ecosystem

denful sits on top of `den` and `flake-aspects`, providing a curated layer of pre configured modules. It's the user facing distribution that makes the Aspect oriented ecosystem accessible to newcomers while remaining composable for advanced users.
