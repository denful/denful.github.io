<Aside type="tip">
[GoDNet](https://github.com/denful/godnet) (Go D-Net) is a Golang implementation of [Delta Interaction Nets](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2505.20314) and compiler backend, built to power [GoDNix](/ecosystem/godnix/).
</Aside>

## What it does

GoDNet implements Delta Interaction Nets in Go, the reduction engine and compiler backend that executes nets produced by the [GoDNix](/ecosystem/godnix/) Nix compiler. Interaction nets are a graph-rewriting model of computation with strong locality and confluence properties, making them a natural target for parallel, optimal reduction.

## Role in the ecosystem

GoDNet is the reduction engine of the Go interaction-net stack. [GoDNix](/ecosystem/godnix/) compiles Nix down to nets, and GoDNet reduces them. It is part of the same line of research as [dnx](/ecosystem/dnx/)'s Δ-Nets runtime.
