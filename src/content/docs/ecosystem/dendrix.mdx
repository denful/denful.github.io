<Aside type="tip">
[dendrix](https://github.com/denful/dendrix) is an aggregator of repositories from the community providing Aspect oriented Nix configurations, akin to initiatives like AUR/NUR, but for Aspect oriented Nix.


 dendrix will soon be renamed as <strong>`DUR`: Denful User Repository.</strong> to clarify its role in the ecosystem.
</Aside>

## What it does

dendrix provides a platform for the community to contribute, discover, and consume Aspect oriented modules hosted on user repositories:

- **Community-submitted**: modules authored and maintained by their original authors from their repositories, no barrier of entry
- **Dendrix Trees**: aspect import-trees from community repositories
- **Ecosystem index**: a catalog of all Aspect oriented projects and libraries

## The Vision

Just as NUR gives the Nix community a place to share packages, dendrix is the place to share **composable configurations**. Instead of copying snippets from dotfiles repos, you import well tested, parameterized aspects that work across NixOS, Darwin, and Home-Manager.

## Current Status

dendrix has the infrastructure in place: CI, templates, documentation, and the ecosystem guide. Its success depends on community participation. **Sponsorship makes the biggest difference here**: it funds dedicated time to build the community, review contributions, and create starter aspects that attract more contributors.
